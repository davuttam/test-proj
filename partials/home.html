COO Speaks

With India being one the fastest growing economies in the world, there is a need to lay the foundation for a strong workforce. To keep up with this pace India needs good quality and quantity of new jobs for the skilled labour.

The government of India, in order to bridge this skill gap, has introduced several programs like the Skill India mission, Start-Up India campaign, Make in India campaign etc. These programs will not only ensure that the existing workforce is imparted with the necessary skills but will make the economy more labour absorbent.

At AITMC – Model Career Centre (MCC), we go beyond just catering to these needs. We look at India’s growth as an opportunity to empower people, improve lives and contribute to the nation-building process. Each and every member of AITMC – MCC shares a common vision of creating and sustaining an environment whereby employment and entrepreneurship can flourish.

The Model Career Centre is the advanced version of the now dwindling Employment Exchanges in India.It must be noted that there are more than 900 employment exchanges in India. According to a census conducted in 2012-13, nearly 44.8 million applicants registered for employment exchange till that year. However, only around 55% of applicants got placed through various Employment Exchanges. Other than Gujarat and Maharashtra all other states have ceased to offer any help to applicants.

The National Service Portal was set up by the Ministry of Labor & Employment (MoLE) to deal with the ineffectiveness as well as the regional imbalance in the performance of the exchanges. AITMC - MCC aims at covering each and every aspect related to employment and provide better opportunities to both youngsters and employers in the job market.
